Balance Assessment - Support & Help

About the App

Circle of Life: Balance Assessment helps you visualize and improve balance across 8 key life categories. Rate your satisfaction, set actionable goals, track milestones, and monitor your progress over time.

Version: 1.0

Tagline: "Where shall we focus?"

Getting Started

1. Rate Your Life Categories

In the Circle tab, you'll see 8 life categories displayed in an interactive wheel:

Health Physical wellness and fitness
Career Professional growth and satisfaction
Finances Financial security and management
Relationships Personal connections and social life
Personal Growth Learning and self-development
Recreation Hobbies and leisure activities
Environment Living space and surroundings
Spirituality Inner peace and life meaning

Adjust the sliders for each category from 0 to 10, where:

  • 0 = Needs significant attention
  • 5 = Moderate satisfaction
  • 10 = Excellent, thriving

You can also interact with the wheel directly by dragging segments to adjust scores. Changes are automatically saved as you adjust them.

2. Set Actions

In the Actions tab, create specific actions to improve categories with lower scores:

  • Tap the "Add Action" button
  • Select a category to focus on
  • Enter your action plan
  • Set a due date (optional)
  • Choose if the action should recur (Daily, Weekly, Monthly, or None)

Actions can be sorted by Created Date or Due Date. Tap an action to edit it, or swipe left to delete.

3. Record Milestones

In the Milestones tab, celebrate your achievements:

  • Tap "Add Milestone"
  • Select the relevant category
  • Describe your milestone
  • Set the positive impact level (High, Medium, or Low)

Milestones can be sorted by Created Date or Impact. Mark them as completed when achieved!

4. Track Your Progress

The History tab shows all your saved assessments over time:

  • Each entry shows your category scores
  • Plus/minus indicators show changes since your last assessment
  • Tap any entry to edit scores
  • View your journey toward a more balanced life

Your first assessment is saved automatically when you adjust scores. Save additional assessments by tapping the save button in the Circle tab.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I save my ratings?

Ratings are automatically saved as you adjust the sliders. No manual save is required for ongoing changes. However, to create a new historical entry, you can explicitly save in the Circle tab.

Can I edit past assessments?

Yes! Tap any entry in the History tab to edit the category scores. The original date is preserved, but you can adjust the ratings.

How do I delete an action or milestone?

Swipe left on any action or milestone card, then tap the delete button. A confirmation prompt will appear.

Can I set recurring actions?

Yes! When creating or editing an action, you can set it to recur Daily, Weekly, Monthly, or None.

Is my data backed up?

All data is stored locally on your device using Core Data. Your data is included in your iCloud backup if you have iCloud Backup enabled. The app does not sync data to external servers.

How do I change the app's appearance?

Go to the Settings tab and choose your preferred color scheme: Auto (follows system), Light, or Dark mode.

Can I delete all my data?

Yes. In Settings, you can:

  • Clear all actions
  • Clear all milestones
  • Erase all responses

These actions cannot be undone, so please be careful.

Does the app work offline?

Yes! The app works completely offline. All data is stored locally on your device.

Is the app available in other languages?

Currently, the app supports English and Spanish. You can change your device language to Spanish to see the app in Spanish.

Troubleshooting

App crashes or freezes

If the app crashes or becomes unresponsive:

  1. Force quit the app (swipe up and swipe away the app)
  2. Restart your device
  3. Reopen the app
  4. If the issue persists, contact support

Data not saving

If your changes aren't being saved:

  1. Check that you have sufficient storage space on your device
  2. Ensure the app has permission to store data (this should be automatic)
  3. Try restarting the app
  4. If problems continue, contact support with details

Scores not updating

If category scores aren't updating:

  1. Try adjusting the slider slightly and releasing
  2. Check that you're on the correct tab
  3. Pull down to refresh if you're in the History tab

Privacy & Data

Circle of Life respects your privacy:

  • All data is stored locally on your device
  • No data is sent to external servers
  • No analytics or tracking
  • No account registration required
  • Your data is included in your standard iOS device backup

The app uses Core Data for local storage and accesses file timestamps for data management purposes only.
