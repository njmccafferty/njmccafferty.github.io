Privacy Policy

Circle of Life: Focus

Last Updated: November 4, 2025

Introduction

Nicolas McCafferty ("we," "our," or "us") operates the Circle of Life: Balance Assessment mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our App.

Important: Circle of Life does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to external servers. All data remains on your device and is private to you.

Information We Do NOT Collect

Circle of Life is designed with privacy as a core principle. We do NOT:

  • Collect personal information
  • Track your usage or behavior
  • Send data to external servers
  • Use analytics or tracking services
  • Share data with third parties
  • Require account registration
  • Collect location data
  • Access your contacts, photos, or other personal data

Data Storage

Local Device Storage

All data created and stored by the App remains on your device:

  • Category Ratings: Your life category scores (0-10) are stored locally
  • Actions: Action items you create are stored on your device
  • Milestones: Milestone records are stored locally
  • History: Historical assessments are stored on your device

Data Storage Technology

The App uses Apple's Core Data framework for local data storage. All data is stored in a local database on your iOS device and is never transmitted over the internet.

iCloud Backup

If you have iCloud Backup enabled on your iOS device, your App data will be included in your standard iOS device backup. This backup is encrypted and stored securely by Apple in accordance with their privacy policies.

API Usage

The App uses the following iOS APIs for essential functionality:

API Category Purpose Reason
File Timestamps Core Data file management C617.1 - Displaying or organizing file timestamps

These APIs are used solely for the App's core functionality and do not involve tracking or data collection.

Third-Party Services

Circle of Life does not integrate with any third-party services, analytics platforms, advertising networks, or social media platforms. The App operates entirely independently.

Children's Privacy

Circle of Life is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. Since we do not collect any personal information, this concern is minimal, but we recommend parental supervision for users under 13.

Data Deletion

You have full control over your data:

  • Delete Individual Items: You can delete individual actions, milestones, or historical entries within the App
  • Clear All Data: In Settings, you can clear all actions, milestones, or responses
  • Uninstall App: Uninstalling the App will remove all data from your device

Please note that deleted data cannot be recovered. Once deleted, it is permanently removed from your device.

Your Rights

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you have complete control:

  • Access: All your data is accessible within the App
  • Modify: You can edit or delete any data at any time
  • Export: Data is included in your iOS device backup
  • Delete: You can delete all data through the App settings or by uninstalling

Security

We take data security seriously:

  • All data is stored locally using Apple's secure Core Data framework
  • Data is protected by iOS device security (Face ID, Touch ID, passcode)
  • No data transmission occurs, eliminating interception risks
  • No external servers means no server-side vulnerabilities

We recommend keeping your iOS device updated with the latest security patches and using device passcode protection.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Last Updated" date at the top of this page indicates when this policy was last revised. Changes will be posted on this page, and if significant changes are made, we will notify users through the App Store update notes.

Your continued use of the App after any changes constitutes acceptance of the updated Privacy Policy.

International Users

Circle of Life is available worldwide. Since all data is stored locally on your device, no data crosses international borders. The App complies with applicable privacy laws, including:

  • General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) - European Union
  • California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) - United States
  • Other applicable regional privacy regulations
